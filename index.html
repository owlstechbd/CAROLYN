WHO AM I?

I am a master of elegance with a refined design sensibility shaped by my education at Parsons School of Design and a career spent among the world’s leaders in luxury fashion and beauty.

From Harper’s Bazaar to Calvin Klein, Chanel / Frédéric Fekkai, and L’Oréal / Maybelline, Bloomingdale’s, I have elevated global brands through intelligent, high-impact storytelling across packaging, print, digital, and experiential design.
I am deeply committed to the arts, having led Beaux Arts South Florida in raising over $2.6 million to support the NSU Art Museum, fostering the next generation of creative thinkers. My passions extend to AI, photography, and equestrian show jumping—disciplines that, like my work, require vision, precision, and an innate sense of elegance. I bring a sophisticated, forward-thinking perspective to every endeavor, creating meaningful, visually compelling experiences that resonate.
